# Faculty Viva Cheatsheet

## One-Line Summary
This monorepo demonstrates enterprise-ready full-stack engineering using React, Node/Express, FastAPI, Spring Boot, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Docker, CI/CD, and observability.

## Fast Answers
- Why SQL and NoSQL? Integrity in SQL, flexible search/content in MongoDB.
- Why saga not 2PC? Lower coupling and better operability across heterogeneous stores.
- How do you prove observability? Prometheus, Grafana, OTEL, Jaeger, correlation ids, metrics endpoints.
- How do you prove security? JWT, RBAC, CSRF, rate limiting, CI security scans.
